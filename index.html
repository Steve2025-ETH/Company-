About Us

Founded in Cannes in 2009, MARQ & PEPPA is a small, independent fashion brand known for its modern yet timeless Parisian style. We value product quality, creativity, and close customer relationships.

Fashion E-commerce Trends (France, 2025)

  • Mobile shopping: 70% of sales come from mobile.
  • Second-hand fashion: 12% of fashion sales in France.
  • Sustainability: Customers prefer eco-friendly brands.
  • Social media: Instagram and TikTok influence shoppers.
